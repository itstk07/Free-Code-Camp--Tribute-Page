1961

On February 2, Barack Obama, Sr., a Kenyan, and Stanley Ann Dunham, an American, married in Maui. On August 4, Barack Jr. was born in Honolulu, Hawaii

1965

Barack’s mother married an Indonesian man, Lolo Soetoro.

1967

Ann and Barack Jr. moved to Lolo Soetoro's hometown of Jakarta, Indonesia.

1979

Barack Jr. started attending college at Occidental College in Los Angeles.

1981

Obama made his first public speech thereafter, he transferred to Columbia University in order to major in political science.

1983

Barack graduated from Columbia University with a Bachelor of Arts degree, magna cum laude.

1988

Barack Obama began to study at Harvard Law School. It was here that he met the future Mrs. Michelle Obama.

1991

Obama graduated from Harvard with his law degree

1992

Obama began to teach constitutional law at the University of Chicago Law School. In October, Obama married Michelle Robinson.

1996

Obama was elected to the Illinois Senate.

1999

Obama Jr. ran for Congress. However, he lost to the Republican Bobby Rush.

2002

Obama started to campaign for the United States Senate.

2004

On July 7, Obama was asked to give a keynote speech at the Democratic National Convention. On November 2, he was elected to the United StatesSenate.

2005

On January 5, Obama was sworn in as a U.S. Senator.

2007

Obama announced his candidacy to become President of the United States.

2008

Obama announced his candidacy to become President of the United States. On November 5, Obama defeated Republican John McCain in the general election and would be the next president.

2011

On April 4, he announced his re-election campaign.

2017

January 20th marked Obama’s last day in office.
